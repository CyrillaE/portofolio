About

Hello! My name is Cyrilla.

When I first received my master's degree in International and European law, I didn't think it was the right path for me.
I chose to embark on a new academic path and earned a bachelor's degree in Art History.
Although I appreciate the theoretical aspects of the subject, something was still lacking until I was introduced to coding!

I learned how to code, designing was for me the way to be rigourous as a lawyer but let express my taste in design, and it started getting better when I discovered the psychology that lead to design with the User Experience!

The field of User Experience is the ideal fusion of psychology and design, working in that sector would be an honour for me.
I felt like I was finally on the correct track after receiving my bachelor's degree in UX design, and my great marks support that feeling!
